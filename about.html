About Scooba Life

The top provider of diving and watersports in the UAE is Scooba Life. We were formed in the UAE since 1995 and were initially created in 1999. With 80 devoted workers, we are the biggest operator in the area. You are about to embark on a world of undersea adventures. In Dubai, scuba diving and adrenaline go hand in hand. With our certified Padi divers and instructors, you can have the best wreck dive, most memorable wall dive, or get the chance to get the ideal shot of an elusive creature, whether you are a seasoned Padi diver or this is your first time.
